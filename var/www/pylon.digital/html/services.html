// 06 — Anycast DNS Hosting

Fast, resilient DNS.
Free with every plan.

Anycast DNS with DNSSEC support hosted on our East African network. Sub-50ms resolution across Kenya and the region. Included at no charge with any Pylon service — or available standalone for domains not hosted with us.

[anycast]
Anycast Routing
DNS queries are answered by the nearest node. Faster resolution and automatic failover if a node goes down.
[dnssec]
DNSSEC Support
Cryptographic signing of DNS records to prevent cache poisoning. We handle key rotation and signing automatically.
[ttl]
Low TTL Support
Minimum 60-second TTL. Useful for failover configurations and rolling deployments.
[api]
DNS API
Manage records programmatically. Supports A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT, SRV, CAA, and PTR records.
[bulk]
Zone Import / Export
Import your existing BIND zone files. Export at any time — no lock-in, no proprietary format.
[kixp]
KIXP-Peered
Our DNS nodes peer at KIXP, so Kenyan ISP customers resolve your domains locally without touching international links.
Free with any VM, managed hosting, email, or VPN plan.  ·  Standalone DNS hosting: KES 150/domain/month, or KES 800/month for up to 10 domains.
