// Setup

From sign-up to
sending in under an hour.

We handle the DNS records and server-side configuration. You just point your domain at us and test your new inbox.

01
Tell us your domain and mailboxes
Share your domain name and the addresses you need — you@yourcompany.co.ke, info@, etc. No minimum, no maximum.
02
Pay first invoice via M-Pesa
We invoice per user per month in KES. Pay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or card. Provisioning begins immediately on payment confirmation.
03
We set up your mailboxes and send DNS records
We create your mailboxes, configure SpamAssassin, DKIM signing, and generate the DNS records you need to add to your domain registrar (e.g. Safaricom, Africa Online, GoDaddy).
04
You add the DNS records at your registrar
Copy the MX, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records we give you into your domain's DNS settings. If you're not sure how, we'll walk you through it — no extra charge.
05
DNS propagates — you're live
DNS typically propagates within 15–30 minutes for .co.ke domains. We verify your MX, DKIM, and DMARC are resolving correctly and confirm when you're good to send.
06
Configure your email client
We send you IMAP/SMTP settings for your preferred client. Most clients support auto-discover. Webmail is available immediately at your Pylon URL.

// DNS records we'll give you

MX — Mail Exchange
Host yourcompany.co.ke
Points to mail.pylon.digital (priority 10)
TXT — SPF Record
Host @
Value v=spf1 include:mail.pylon.digital ~all
TXT — DKIM Signature
Host pylon._domainkey.yourcompany.co.ke
Value v=DKIM1; k=rsa; p=MIIBIjAN... (we generate this)
TXT — DMARC Policy
Host _dmarc.yourcompany.co.ke
Value v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@pylon.digital

We'll give you these exact values for your domain. If your registrar is unfamiliar, we'll walk you through where to paste them.

// FAQ

Email questions.

Can't find your answer? Email us or check the full FAQ.

Can I keep my existing emails when I switch to Pylon?+

Yes. We can migrate your existing email history from your current provider — whether that's Gmail, cPanel, Outlook, or another IMAP server. We connect to your old mailbox, copy all messages and folder structure to the new mailbox, and you lose nothing.

Migration takes a few hours depending on mailbox size. We do it before you change your MX records so your old email keeps arriving until the switch is complete.

Can I use Outlook, Apple Mail, or the Gmail app?+

Yes — any IMAP or POP3 client works. We provide IMAP and SMTP server settings for you to configure in your client of choice. Most modern clients also support auto-discover, which can configure settings automatically from your email address.

Common clients: Outlook (Windows/Mac), Apple Mail (macOS/iOS), Thunderbird, Gmail app (via IMAP), Android Mail, Spark, Airmail. If your client supports IMAP, it works.

What are email aliases and how many can I have?+

An alias is an additional email address that delivers to an existing mailbox. For example, info@yourco.co.ke, sales@yourco.co.ke, and hello@yourco.co.ke can all deliver to a single you@yourco.co.ke mailbox — no extra charge, no limit on the number.

Aliases are managed via webmail or by emailing us. They're useful for role-based addresses that don't need a separate login.

Why does SPF/DKIM/DMARC matter for my business?+

SPF tells receiving servers which mail servers are allowed to send email for your domain — preventing others from spoofing your address. DKIM cryptographically signs your outgoing email so the receiving server can verify it wasn't tampered with. DMARC specifies what to do if SPF or DKIM fails — and sends you reports about it.

Without these, your emails are more likely to land in spam — especially at large providers like Gmail and Outlook. We configure all three as part of setup, which is something many hosting providers skip.

What happens if I receive spam or a phishing email?+

SpamAssassin and ClamAV scan all inbound messages. Spam is scored and flagged — messages above your threshold are moved to your Junk folder. If something gets through that shouldn't, you can mark it as spam in webmail to train the filter. If something is being incorrectly flagged as spam, email us and we'll investigate.

Is my email encrypted?+

All connections to our mail server — IMAP, SMTP, and webmail — are encrypted with TLS. Email in transit between mail servers is encrypted via STARTTLS where supported by the receiving server. Email at rest (stored on our servers) is on encrypted storage in our Nairobi facility.

Note: end-to-end encryption (S/MIME, PGP) is not configured automatically — if your organisation requires end-to-end encryption for specific communications, that's configured at the client level. Ask us if you need guidance.

Can I send bulk marketing emails through Pylon?+

Not through the standard mailbox plans — these are for transactional and business email. Sending bulk marketing email from a standard IMAP mailbox risks blacklisting the sending IP, which harms all customers on the same server.

Our SMTP Relay — High Volume plan is designed for bulk sending with a dedicated IP and proper warmup. For newsletter volumes above 50,000/day, we'd recommend a dedicated sending service like Mailgun or Amazon SES — and we're happy to help you set one up. See our AUP for the full anti-spam policy.

Can I add more mailboxes later without disruption?+

Yes — email us with the new address and we'll create it and add it to your next invoice. New mailboxes are available within a few hours of your request. No DNS changes required after initial setup — new addresses on the same domain just work.
