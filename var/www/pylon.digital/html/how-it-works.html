// The Process

Seven steps. No surprises.

Here is exactly what happens between "hello" and having infrastructure you can rely on.

01
Contact

Tell us what you're trying to build.

Send us an email, fill in the contact form, or message us on WhatsApp. You don't need a polished brief — just tell us roughly what you need.

  • What's the workload? (web app, API, database, mail server…)
  • How many users or requests do you expect?
  • Any compliance or data-residency requirements?
  • What's your rough monthly budget in KES?
⏱ Your side — 5 minutes
// How to reach us

Email us at hello@pylon.digital, use the contact form on the homepage, or WhatsApp +254 700 000 000. We reply in plain language — no jargon, no sales script.

→ Response within 1 business day
02
Consultation

We scope your requirements together.

One of our engineers will follow up — by email, call, or WhatsApp — to clarify your needs and make sure we recommend the right solution. We'll ask a few technical questions, but we'll explain everything in plain terms.

  • We discuss VM specs, storage, and networking needs
  • For colo: rack space, power draw, uplink requirements
  • We flag if your workload needs something we can't do well
  • We suggest the minimum viable spec — not the most expensive
⏱ Usually one email round or a 15-min call
// Our promise here

If we're not the right fit for what you need — scale, multi-region, managed Kubernetes — we'll tell you that honestly and point you in the right direction. We'd rather lose a sale than set you up to fail.

→ No overselling. Ever.
03
Quote

You receive a clear, itemised quote in KES.

We send you a PDF quote with every line item spelled out. No hidden fees, no ambiguous "usage charges." The number on the quote is the number on your invoice.

  • Price in KES (USD equivalent if preferred)
  • Monthly recurring cost clearly separated from one-time setup
  • Any optional add-ons shown as separate line items
  • Quote valid for 14 days
⏱ Quote delivered within 24 hours

04
Payment

First invoice paid — however works for you.

We issue the first invoice and you pay it via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or card. Once payment clears, provisioning begins. No upfront contracts required for month-to-month plans.

  • M-Pesa Till or Paybill — instant confirmation
  • KCB / Equity / Co-op bank transfer
  • Credit or debit card (Visa / Mastercard)
  • Invoice receipt sent automatically on payment
⏱ M-Pesa clears instantly — bank transfer same day
// Billing cycle

Your first payment covers a full calendar month from the date of provisioning. Subsequent invoices are sent on the 1st of each month, due by the 7th. No auto-deductions without your approval.

→ Always billed in arrears from month 2
05
Provisioning

We build and configure your infrastructure.

Our team provisions your VM or colo slot, applies your requested OS and configuration, sets up networking and IP assignments, and runs pre-handover checks.

  • OS installed and updated (your choice of distro)
  • SSH key authentication configured (password login disabled)
  • IPv4 and/or IPv6 assigned and tested
  • Reverse DNS set on request
  • Firewall baseline applied; you adjust from there
  • Snapshot or backup configured if included
⏱ VMs: 4–24 hours · Colo: 24–72 hours
// What we send you

A secure handover document containing your IP address, SSH login details, control panel credentials (if applicable), and a brief network topology summary. Sent via encrypted email.

→ No default passwords. SSH keys only.
06
Handover

Credentials and documentation delivered.

You receive everything you need to take over and start deploying. We do a brief walkthrough call or message exchange — whichever you prefer — to make sure you're oriented.

  • SSH/SFTP credentials and IP addresses
  • DNS configuration guide for your domain
  • Link to our network status and abuse contact
  • Direct WhatsApp and email contact for your account
  • Optional: 30-minute orientation call with an engineer
⏱ Handover same day as provisioning completion
// New customer check-in

We follow up 72 hours after handover to make sure everything is running as expected and answer any early questions. Not a sales call — just making sure you're good.

→ 72-hour check-in included for all new customers
07
Ongoing

We monitor, you build. We're here when you need us.

Once you're live, Pylon monitors the network, power, and physical infrastructure continuously. We handle NOC-level incidents. You focus on your product.

  • Network uptime monitored 24/7
  • Status updates at status.pylon.digital
  • Monthly invoice on the 1st — pay by the 7th
  • Scale up or add services with a single message
  • Cancel any time — no penalties, 30 days' notice
⏱ Month-to-month from here
// What you get ongoing

Reach your account contact directly via WhatsApp or email for anything from billing questions to network troubleshooting. No ticket queue for existing customers.

→ Direct line to a Nairobi engineer. Always.

How billing works

No surprise charges

Your monthly cost is fixed. We don't do metered egress, per-request billing, or "consumption-based" pricing that requires a spreadsheet to understand.

Invoices on the 1st

Monthly invoices land in your inbox on the 1st of each month. Payment is due by the 7th. We send one reminder if a payment is late before suspending anything.

Prorated first month

If you start mid-month, your first invoice covers only the remaining days — calculated to the day. No paying for time you didn't use.

VAT included

All prices include 16% VAT. What you see is what you pay. We issue ETR receipts for all M-Pesa payments and formal tax invoices on request.

pylon-support — common questions
what happens if my VM goes down at 2am?
Check status.pylon.digital first. If it's a network event, our NOC is already working it. If it's an issue with your specific VM (not network-wide), use the emergency contact in your handover document. We respond to network-level incidents around the clock.
can I upgrade my VM without losing data?
Yes. Send us a message and we'll schedule an upgrade window with you — usually within 24 hours. Most upgrades require a brief reboot (under 5 minutes). We take a snapshot before any changes.
how do I cancel?
Email or WhatsApp us with 30 days' notice. We'll confirm the end date, make sure you've exported any data you need, and close the account cleanly. No cancellation fees. No guilt trips.
do you handle server administration for me?
VMs are self-managed — we handle the hardware, network, and power; you manage the OS and software. Managed hosting customers get OS-level support included. If you need managed sysadmin for a self-managed VM, ask us — we quote separately.
